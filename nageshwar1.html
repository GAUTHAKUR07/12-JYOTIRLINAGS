Nageshwar Temple - The Powerful Shiva Shrine

The Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, located near **Dwarka, Gujarat, is considered a **symbol of protection from evil forces. It is believed that *Lord Shiva appeared here to save his devotee Supriya* from a demon named *Daaruka*.

The *Shivling at Nageshwar* is unique as it faces south, unlike most Jyotirlingas. The temple also houses a *huge 80-feet statue of Lord Shiva*, visible from a great distance.
