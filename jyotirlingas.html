12 Jyotirlingas of India – Short Stories

1. Somnath Jyotirlinga (Gujarat)

Legend says that the Moon God (Chandra) was cursed by his father-in-law, Daksha. Lord Shiva restored him, making this the first and most sacred Jyotirlinga.

2. Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga (Andhra Pradesh)

It is believed that Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati visited the site to console their son Kartikeya, who had chosen to live alone on Mount Krauncha.

3. Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga (Madhya Pradesh)

King Chandrasena was saved from a demon by Lord Shiva, who emerged from the ground as the fierce Mahakaleshwar.

4. Omkareshwar Jyotirlinga (Madhya Pradesh)

Lord Shiva appeared in the form of Omkareshwar after the prayers of the Vindhya mountain, who sought forgiveness for his sins.

5. Kedarnath Jyotirlinga (Uttarakhand)

After the Kurukshetra war, the Pandavas sought forgiveness from Lord Shiva, who took the form of a bull and later appeared as the Kedarnath Jyotirlinga.

6. Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga (Maharashtra)

Lord Shiva destroyed the demon Bhima and settled as Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga, blessing the region with peace and prosperity.

7. Kashi Vishwanath Jyotirlinga (Uttar Pradesh)

Lord Shiva promised to stay in Kashi and bless all those who visit and worship him with liberation (moksha).

8. Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga (Maharashtra)

Lord Shiva emerged at Trimbak to grant salvation to Sage Gautam and bless the origin of the holy river Godavari.

9. Vaidyanath Jyotirlinga (Jharkhand)

Ravana offered his ten heads to Lord Shiva at this place, earning his blessing but also his downfall due to pride.

10. Nageshwar Jyotirlinga (Gujarat)

Lord Shiva appeared as Nageshwar to rescue Supriya, a devotee trapped by a demon named Daaruka.

11. Rameshwar Jyotirlinga (Tamil Nadu)

Lord Rama installed the Shivalinga here and prayed to Lord Shiva for victory against Ravana.

12. Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga (Maharashtra)

Lord Shiva blessed a devotee named Ghushma, who showed immense faith even after her son was killed by her sister.
