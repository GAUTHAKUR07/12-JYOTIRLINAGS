Trimbakeshwar Temple - The Origin of Godavari

The Trimbakeshwar Temple, located in **Nashik, Maharashtra, is one of the **most sacred Jyotirlingas. It is famous for being the origin of the holy Godavari River and is a major pilgrimage site.

Unlike other Jyotirlingas, the Trimbakeshwar Shivling has three faces representing *Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva*. The temple is built in classic Hemadpanthi architecture and is surrounded by the *Brahmagiri Hills*.
