Grishneshwar Temple - The Sacred Shiva Shrine

The Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga, located near **Ellora, Maharashtra, is the **last of the twelve Jyotirlingas*. It is said that a devoted woman named *Kusuma built this temple* after Lord Shiva blessed her.

This temple is *one of the smallest Jyotirlingas*, yet it holds immense religious significance due to its *historical and spiritual importance*.
