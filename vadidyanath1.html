Vaidyanath Temple - The Temple of Healing

The Vaidyanath Jyotirlinga, located in **Deoghar, Jharkhand, is one of the most powerful Jyotirlingas. It is believed that Lord Shiva manifested here as the "Supreme Healer" (Vaidya) to cure ailments and grant Moksha.
