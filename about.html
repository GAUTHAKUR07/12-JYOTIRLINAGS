About Jyotirlingas

Jyotirlingas are sacred shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva, representing his divine light. There are twelve Jyotirlingas spread across India, each holding a unique story and spiritual significance. It is believed that visiting these shrines helps devotees attain spiritual peace and liberation. The twelve Jyotirlingas are Somnath, Mallikarjuna, Mahakaleshwar, Omkareshwar, Kedarnath, Bhimashankar, Kashi Vishwanath, Trimbakeshwar, Vaidyanath, Nageshwar, Rameshwar, and Grishneshwar.
