Mallikarjuna Temple - The Divine Mountain Shrine

The Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga, located in **Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh*, is known as the *Kailash of the South*. It is one of the rare temples where *both Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati* reside.

üèî Unique Feature

This is the only Jyotirlinga where *Shakti Peetha and Jyotirlinga* exist together, making it an extremely *sacred pilgrimage site* for both Shaivites and Shaktas.

üìÖ Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mallikarjuna is from October to March, with *Mahashivratri and Navratri* being the most important festivals here.
